About

Felon Friendlii Resources is a community-maintained directory that helps people with felony records find housing and supportive services that welcome them.

This site is a lightweight static build using plain HTML, CSS, and a tiny bit of JavaScript—no frameworks, no build tools. It’s fast, accessible, and privacy-friendly.

How you can help

  • Know a landlord, program, or service that’s felon‑friendly? Submit a resource.
  • Share the site with people and organizations who might benefit.

Notes

Listings are provided as-is and may change. Always contact providers directly to confirm current policies and availability.
